Our Beginning

The Genesis of Our Mission

"From personal struggle comes the strongest determination to create change."

According to Benite Mukombo, our foundation's story begins with his own childhood experiences in Lubumbashi. Growing up in a modest home with his parents and three siblings, his family often struggled with basic necessities, sometimes going to bed hungry due to financial hardships.

At just 9 years old, Benite and his siblings were selling garden vegetables roadside to help their family survive - while other children enjoyed their childhood, they were occupied with helping their parents make ends meet.

These early experiences instilled in Benite a deep empathy for families facing poverty and hardship, particularly understanding the trauma parents experience when unable to provide for their children.
