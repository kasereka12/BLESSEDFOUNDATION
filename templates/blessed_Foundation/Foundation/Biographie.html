Childhood & Education

Benite Mukombo was born in Mbuji-Mayi on October 5th in the Democratic Republic of Congo. She grew up in Katanga, Lubumbashi, where she completed her secondary education in math and physics at Kazi-Bora. After obtaining her baccalaureate in 2016, she spent 3 years at the Higher Institute of Statistics studying management informatics. Passionate about aviation, she traveled to the United States to further her studies and become a pilot.


Journey

In 2022, she began writing her first book "FROM HATE TO LOVE," which chronicles her traumatic hellish experience and how she emerged from it. This was followed by her second book, "A WOMAN A QUEEN," where she discusses the characteristics and value of a queen. On February 15, 2024, she expressed her passion for helping women through her movement "I AM A QUEEN," which aims to help women thrive and find their inner light, inspiring each other through love, kindness, happiness, and faith. In December 2023, she made an excellent connection with LMCTV, which introduced her to the media world, an area she had never considered before. Today, being in the virtual world, she plans to venture into cinema to enlighten people through her films. She is an entrepreneur, CEO, and founder of Blessed Foundation, which focuses on empowering women, supporting orphans and underprivileged children, and aiding widows and single mothers who bear sole responsibility for parenting by showing them the path to salvation through Jesus Christ. Benite Mukombo is a Christian missionary with a mission to evangelize. - Journalist at LMCTV - Speaker and motivator
